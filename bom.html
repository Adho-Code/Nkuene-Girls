Board of Managements

I am delighted to participate together with the team of stakeholders of Nkuene Girls’ High School in the development of the website. The website has outlined the blueprint for Nkuene Girls’ High School to progressively develop and implement relevant institutional programmers for continued growth. As parents and teachers of Nkuene Girls’ High School our main objective is to achieve high academic excellence accompanied by high values of discipline, hard work, honesty, team work, attention to detail and assertiveness. The website shall attempt to achieve these noble objectives so that we can have an all round learner and therefore, a better society. The role of parents as a pillar of resource mobilization and a major stakeholder has been clearly outlined for their effective participation at various levels. The website will meet targets and improve performance index in resource mobilization, utilization, accountability and responsibility at all levels of institutional administration and governance. To achieve our desired goals, we intend to;.... 1: Involve all the relevant stakeholders. 2: Adequately utilize and improve on existing regulatory framework and in-corporate acceptable best practices. 3: Progressively modernize existing institutional infrastructure by appropriate utilization of available resources for the benefit of students and staff. 4: Empower institutional personnel at all levels by ensuring continuous training, infrastructure improvement (Equipment, housing, work space, environment) and a supportive administration. 5: Develop and implement strategic alliances and networks at all levels to realize our objectives. On behalf of all the parents and teachers, I wish to thank the school for the installation of a website.

Welcome to the Nkuene Girls' High School Parents Teachers Association website. This is one forum for parents, teachers and students to interact with each other in our continuous drive for improvement. Any such improvements will ultimately benefit our children and help provide a conducive environment for them to conduct their studies. This website will also provide you with updates on the activities of the PTA and will provide you with the opportunity to give us feedback on-line on ideas / suggestions to improve. As such , we would greatly appreciate it if you would spend some of your time to give us your feedback / input by clicking on the e-mail button on this page. Please take this as an opportunity to raise issues / problems that you may feel have not been given the right amount of attention or in general just to share your views. We promise that we will take into account all contributions, and will table these for discussion in the PTA meeting. We will ensure that any outcome or follow up from your contributions will be published in timely manner. Finally, on behalf of the PTA, I'd like to express many thanks to the institution that helped us in creating this website. We look forward to hearing your views and working with you. GOD Bless You all Splendidly.
