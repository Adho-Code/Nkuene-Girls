    Principal's Message

    Welcome to the Nkuene Girls' High School website. As you read through the pages of this site, you will learn about our wonderful students, our School, and get a feel for the vibrancy and strength of our wider Nkuene Girls' High School community. Nkuene Girls' High School is one of the leading providers of senior secondary education in the North eastern Territory. We aim to give every student in our care the very best possible education in order to prepare them for life beyond school. We are a ‘Learning Community’ which encourages staff, students, past students and parents to involve themselves fully in all aspects of school life and to identify themselves with its core values of Teamwork, Hard work, Integrity and Professionalism. Our school is large enough to provide a broad and differentiated curriculum that caters for varied interests and abilities....Nkuene Girls’ High School has a very strong and supportive School Council that ensures that the school continues to excel in everything we do. Additionally, we have structured our management team so that every year level has Form Masters and Careers Masters who manage every individual student and assist them in their journey throughout their senior secondary schooling life and beyond. Our educational program is wide, varied and our record of success in the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) results is outstanding. Students find that they can pursue their interests and strengths to the best of their ability, and are encouraged to achieve their personal best. We are proud of our record and of our success in helping students achieve their dreams once they leave school. At the same time, our vision of education extends far beyond the classroom. We encourage our students to involve themselves in the broad co-curricular programme. Learning here takes place in many shapes and forms. Students have the opportunity to participate fully on the sporting field, on the stage, in leadership activities, Vocational Education and Training courses and on the Debating platform. We expect high standards in all that we do at Nkuene Girls' High School. We encourage our students to aim high in their studies, to work hard and to be sensitive to the rights and needs of others. In Nkuene Girls' we are all Players , no spectators are allowed, of course, this website cannot take the place of visiting our School. I warmly welcome you to visit our School by contacting us, and discover firsthand what makes a Nkuene Girls’ High School education so special.

    The Principal’s desk:
    Mrs.Dinah.W. Gitonga.

    Admission

    'Nkuene Girls’ high school maintains high standards of discipline both personal and academic.The school has a discipline committee constitutes of the Principal, the Deputy Principal and two other teachers working in tandem with all members of the teaching staff.The school does not entertain idlers. In fact school rule numbers 15 stipulates that all students are expected to pass all the exams given by the school. This is in line with our school mission i.e. to provide quality education and inculcate moral values for sustaina ble societal development. From the year 2011 Nkuene Girls has kept the trophy for the most disciplined school in the district..
